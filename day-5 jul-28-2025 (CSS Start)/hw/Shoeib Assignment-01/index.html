THE KOKHATIEL

Cockatiels are small, crested parrots native to Australia, known for their friendly and affectionate nature, making them popular pets. They are relatively easy to care for and breed, so quite, modest,friendly,so beautiful making them a good choice for both novice and experienced bird owners.

THE MACAW

Macaws are large, colorful parrots known for their intelligence and powerful beaks. They are native to Central and South America and inhabit tropical rainforests and other wooded areas. Macaws are social birds that live in flocks,quite loud, using vocalizations to communicate.

THE PIGEON

Pigeons are gentle, plump, small-billed birds with a skin saddle (cere) between the bill and forehead. All pigeons strut about with a characteristic bobbing of the head. Because of their long wings and powerful flight muscles, they are strong, swift fliers. Pigeons are monogamous; i.e., they mate for life, and the survivor accepts a new mate only slowly.
